Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Neural Puzzle ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application Neural Puzzle (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

TL;DR: We don't collect ANY of your personal data. Neural Puzzle is completely offline and privacy-first.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Neural Puzzle is designed as a completely offline application. We want to be absolutely clear about what we do NOT collect:

Personal Information

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers
  • We do NOT collect your device's contact list, photos, or other personal files
  • We do NOT collect your location data or GPS coordinates
  • We do NOT collect biometric data or facial recognition information

Usage Data

  • We do NOT track your app usage patterns or gameplay statistics
  • We do NOT collect information about how long you play or which features you use
  • We do NOT collect crash reports or technical diagnostics
  • We do NOT collect device information such as device ID, operating system version, or hardware specifications

Game Data

  • Your game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only
  • We do NOT sync your game data to external servers
  • We do NOT backup your game data to cloud services
  • We do NOT share your scores or achievements with third parties

Network Activity

  • The app does NOT connect to the internet
  • We do NOT send any data to external servers
  • We do NOT receive any data from external sources
  • The app functions completely offline

  • Analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)

Data Storage

All game-related data (such as your current puzzle state, best scores, and game settings) is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard local storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • Is not backed up to iCloud or other cloud services unless you specifically enable iOS device backups
